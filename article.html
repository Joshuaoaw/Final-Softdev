Cebu Technological University Danao Campus: A Hub for Innovation and Excellence.

Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus is a premier educational institution in northern Cebu, recognized for its commitment to fostering innovation, academic excellence, and community engagement Nestled in the vibrant city of Danao. The campus is home to over 4,000 students who are dedicated to lifelong learning and career development in various fields of study, particularly in engineering, technology, and education.
